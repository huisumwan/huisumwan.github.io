Introduction

History is one of the most difficult subjects for students to learn. There are so many facts to memorize and understand; from historical figures to battle dates. Listening to lectures and reading textbooks can be boring and ineffective. Students need to be able to assess the information at their own pace, and 3DLE will be the best way to achieve their goals.

This program is designed to present middle school students with a straightforward approach to Ancient History. They will be able to choose a country and search important events and time periods. Their search will lead them to the topic of their choice, and from there they will have access to a study guide and a quiz.

3DLE will benefit teachers as well as students. Teachers will be able to tailor their lesson plan to the information provided by the program. They will be able to request access to their students account to confirm completion of all assigned readings and quizzes. This program can be a used at home and in the classroom, which allows for flexibility.
